Accidental Injuries:

The first area they are concerned with is when you are injured in any type of accident. Have either one of you thought about what you will do when you get injured in an accident? Well, the Labor Advisory Board and American Income have given it a lot of thought.

Their concern isn't that your medical bills won't be taken care of. You have health insurance to take care of that. When it's job related, workers compensation is there, and a lot of members have some form of disability insurance as well. But the major concerns is your lost wages and all of the other expenses that start adding up. when you end up in the hospital, even though your medical bills are taken care of, no one is paying you to be there, right? when you get that phone call, you won't be working either. You'll be THERE by his side and vice versa. God forbid anything happens to one of the children, you'll definitely take time off work to be by their side, right? Because of that, your benefit plan will pay money directly to you when you , or the children are receiving treatment for any kind of accident.
